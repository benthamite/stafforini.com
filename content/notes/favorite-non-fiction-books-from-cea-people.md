## James Ting-Edwards {#james-ting-edwards}

-   _Thinking: fast and slow_, by Daniel Kahneman

A deep but accessible overview of important findings in heuristics and biases, and how these are relevant to everyday decisions.

-   _Made to stick_, by Chip Heath and Dan Heath

Tells you how to present ideas as compelling stories, which people will notice and remember.

-   _Good and real_, by Gary Drescher

An AI researcher takes on philosophy, arguing that we can act even in a universe of causal laws, and that reciprocal altruism is rational even for selfish agents.

-   _Gödel, Escher, Bach: An eternal golden braid_, Douglas Hofstadter

Whimsically explores logical systems through colourful dialogues, games, and historical excursions.

-   _The concept of law_, H. L. A. Hart

Presents law as a social institution, open to empirical study though it engages with moral concerns.
