## James Ting-Edwards

- *Thinking: fast and slow*, by Daniel Kahneman
  A deep but accessible overview of important findings in heuristics and biases, and how these are relevant to everyday decisions.
- *Made to stick*, by Chip Heath and Dan Heath
  Tells you how to present ideas as compelling stories, which people will notice and remember.
- *Good and real*, by Gary Drescher
  An AI researcher takes on philosophy, arguing that we can act even in a universe of causal laws, and that reciprocal altruism is rational even for selfish agents.
