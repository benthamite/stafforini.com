Here's a list of all the apps currently installed on my Android smartphone. My favorite ones are **boldfaced**. Please note that a few apps require root access; if your phone is not rooted, these apps will not work.

Let me know if you think I'm missing anything. I'm interested both in superior alternatives to the apps I currently use and in completely novel apps.

- [7 Minute Workout](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.minhphan.android.seven). A simple, minimal exercise routine.
- **[AdAway](https://f-droid.org/repository/browse/?fdid=org.adaway)**. An open source ad blocker for Android using the hosts file. It needs Android >= 2.1 and ROOT access.
- [Airbnb](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.airbnb.android).
- [Amazon](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.amazon.mShop.android.shopping).
- [Android Pay](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.walletnfcrel).
- **[AnkiDroid](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ichi2.anki)**. Review your Anki decks remotely with this excellent app.
- [Anti SMS Spam](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.baole.app.antismsspam). Prevent unwanted messages from any sender.
- [AppBlock](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=cz.mobilesoft.appblock). Blocks distracting apps, like Facebook.
- [Asana](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.asana.app). For task-management.
- **[Audible](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.audible.application)**. A normal day contains plenty of activities that don't occupy one's attention, such as walking, washing the dishes, brushing one's teeth, etc., which are ideal for listening to audiobooks.
- [Authenticator Plus](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mufri.authenticatorplus). Better than Google Authenticator.
- [BodySpace](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bodybuilding.mobile). Useful (though imperfect) app for tracking gym workouts.
- [CamScanner](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.intsig.camscanner). The best app I found for scanning documents with the phone's camera.
- [Calendar](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.calendar&hl=en). I've tried other calendar apps, but if you use Google Calendar on your computer it's just easier to keep everything sync with the native Google Android app.
- [Chrome](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.android.chrome). The mobile version of Chrome used to be very unstable, but it has gotten much better recently.
- [Click Counter](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=digitalfish.counter). Useful for running various experiments.
- [Coffitivity](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.coffitivity.coffitivity). Recreates the ambient noises of a cafe.
- [Compass](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.gn.android.compass). For use in combination with Google Maps, in cases where the GPS signal is lost or there is no internet connection.  An underrated tool.
- [Couchsurfing](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.couchsurfing.mobile.android).
- [DiskUsage](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.diskusage). Generates a spatial representation of the contents of the phone's storage card.
- [Drive](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.docs). My entire (~1.7 TB) drive is on Google Drive; with this app I can access any of these files from my phone.
- [Drive Autosync](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ttxapps.drivesync). I use this app to synchronize my entire PDF library (comprising about 6,000 files) between my phone and my computer.
- [Duolingo](https://play.google.com/store/apps/developer?id=Duolingo). A good app for learning languages.
- [ES File Explorer](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.estrongs.android.pop). A good file-management app.
- **[ezPDF reader](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=udk.android.reader)**. My favorite PDF reader.
- [Feedly](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.devhd.feedly). As good as the now-defunct Google Reader.
- [Foursquare](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.joelapenna.foursquared). For local recommendations.
- **[Gmail](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.gm)**.
- [Goodreads](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.goodreads&hl=en). I mostly use Goodreads on my computer, but occasionally use this app to check things on my phone.
- **[Google maps](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.maps)**.
- [Google translate](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.translate).
- [Google trips](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.travel.onthego).
- [gStrings](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.cohortor.gstrings&hl=en). An excellent tuner. Will recognize any pitch frequency, on any instrument.
- [Habits](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.isoron.uhabits). For keeping track of dailies.
- [Hangouts](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.talk&hl=en). On a couple of occasions I had trouble using Hangouts on my computer, but still managed to talk thanks to this app.
- [Hoy Milonga](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.hoy). An indispensable app for tango aficionados.
- [Instagram](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.instagram.android). For sharing pictures.
- [**Instapaper**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.instapaper.android). The best app of its kind; the text-to-speech feature is invaluable.
- [Kayak](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kayak.android). Recommended for frequent travelers.  Recently I missed a flight to Oslo and managed to find another one quickly enough thanks to this app.
- [**Keep**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.keep). Great note-taking app.
- [Last.fm](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=fm.last.android&hl=en). I use it only to scrobble the music I play on my phone.
- [LoungeBuddy](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.loungebuddy.playstore&hl=en). Enter all your cards and this app will tell you which airport lounges you can access. invaluable for nomads and frequent travelers.
- [Lyft](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=me.lyft.android).
- [Memrise](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.memrise.android.memrisecompanion). Spaced-repetition app; supplements Anki.
- [Messenger](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.facebook.orca). This app actually makes it easier for me to spend less time browsing Facebook, since I can answer messages without having to log in to the site.
- [Mycelium](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mycelium.wallet). Bitcoin wallet.
- [Mytaxi](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=taxi.android.client).
- [Nova launcher](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.teslacoilsw.launcher). Improves the home screen.
- **[Podcast addict](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bambuna.podcastaddict)**. Excellent podcast app.
- **[Pushbullet](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pushbullet.android)**. Send SMS messages from your computer and see phone notifications on your desktop browser.
- [Random number generator](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ru.uxapps.random).
- [RealCalc Scientific Calculator](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=uk.co.nickfines.RealCalc). The native Android calculator sucks; use this one instead.
- [RescueTime](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.rescuetime.android). I mainly track time manually with Toggl, though I've found it useful to have an automatic time tracker as well, which I use on both my laptop and smartphone.
- [Revolut](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.revolut.revolut). A virtual bank/credit card that lets me pay in foreign currencies without fees or unfavorable exchange rates.
- [Rocker locker](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tomwandroid.rockerlocker). Make volume keys control media volume by default.
- [Ruler](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=fr.ecp.ruler.app).
- [Seconds Free](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.runloop.seconds.free&hl=en). I want an app that vibrates regularly at custom intervals, that vibrates for long enough for me to notice, and that doesn't drain my phone's battery. I tried about eight or nine apps; this is the best one I found.
- [Shazaam](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shazam.android). Will recognize most music playing in one's environment.
- [Signal](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.thoughtcrime.securesms). Secure communication.
- [Slack](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.Slack).
- [Sleep as Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.urbandroid.sleep). Smart alarm clock with sleep cycle tracking.
- **[Smart AudioBook Player](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ak.alizandro.smartaudiobookplayer&hl=en)**. Are you looking for an audio player that allows you to play audiobooks at faster speeds without pitch alteration or sound distortion? This is the one.
- [Smart Voice Recorder](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.andrwq.recorder). A good voice recorder.
- [Splitwise](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.Splitwise.SplitwiseMobile). For splitting bills and expenses.
- [Spotify](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.spotify.music).
- [TagTime](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=bsoule.tagtime). Beeps at random times. A handy tool for self-experimentation.
- [Tasker](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.dinglisch.android.taskerm). Automate all sorts of tasks.
- [Titanium Backup](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.keramidas.TitaniumBackup). A powerful backup tool.
- **[Toggl](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.toggl.timer)**. By far the best time-tracking app I'm familiar with.
- [**Toshl**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.thirdframestudios.android.expensoor). The best app for managing finances I found.
- [TripAdvisor](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.tripadvisor.tripadvisor).
- [Twilight](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.urbandroid.lux). F.lux equivalent for Android.
- [Uber](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ubercab).
- [WhatsApp](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.whatsapp).
- [Weather Underground](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wunderground.android.weather). Hyper-local weather forecasts.
- [YouTube](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.youtube).
