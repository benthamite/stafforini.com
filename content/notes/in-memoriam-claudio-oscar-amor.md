Superlative verbal skills

Nozick. Substantively: also a libertarian, but less so as time went by. Methodologically: "non-coercive" approach to philosophy. Personally: he had once become deeply disillusioned with philosophy, and it was reading _Anarchy, state and utopia_ that rekindled his interest in the discipline.
