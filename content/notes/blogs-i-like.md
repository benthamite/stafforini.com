These are some of the blogs I like.  When this is not otherwise obvious, author names for individual blogs, or for group blogs with a primary author, appear in parentheses.  If you think I'm missing something I would enjoy, please let me know. **All-time favorites are boldfaced**.

-   [80,000 Hours](http://80000hours.org/blog). The official blog of a nonprofit dedicated to figuring out how people can do the most good with their careers.
-   [**Andart II**](http://aleph.se/andart2/) (Anders Sandberg). A blog by someone who holds the rare distinction of being one of the most impressive polymaths out there and one of the happiest people alive.
-   [Astral Codex Ten](https://astralcodexten.substack.com/). Much more than you wanted to know about almost any topic.
-   [Bet on It (Bryan Caplan)](https://betonit.substack.com/).
-   [Dan Luu](https://danluu.com/).
-   [Fantastic Anachronism](https://fantasticanachronism.com) (Álvaro de Menard). Hard to categorize. See for yourself.
-   [Fin Moorhouse](https://www.finmoorhouse.com/writing/). Co-host of the excellent podcast [Hear This Idea](https://hearthisidea.com/), _inter alia._
-   [Giving gladly](http://www.givinggladly.com/) (Julia Wise). "Why well-planned giving is the most important thing you can do --- and more fun than you thought."
-   [Information Processing](http://infoproc.blogspot.com/) (Steve Hsu). Writings on cognitive genomics and other stuff. He co-hosts the podcast Manifold, also strongly recommended.
-   [Jeff Kaufman](http://www.jefftk.com/index). Writings on a wide-range of topics.
-   [Jethro Kuan](https://blog.jethro.dev/). Creator of [my favorite Emacs package](https://github.com/org-roam/org-roam).
-   [Meteuphoric](http://meteuphoric.wordpress.com/) (Katja Grace). Musings about "human behavior, social institutions and rules, anthropic reasoning, personal experimentation and improvement, philanthropy, and the prospect of robots replacing humans."
-   [Nintil](https://nintil.com/) (José Luis Ricón). "To estimate, compare, distinguish, discuss, and trace to its principal sources everything."
-   [Overcoming bias](http://www.overcomingbias.com/) (Robin Hanson). "No one is smarter than Robin Hanson." -- Bryan Caplan.
-   [Philosophical multicore](http://mdickens.me/) (Michael Dickens). Insightful posts about some of the most important questions.
-   [Philosophy, et cetera](http://www.philosophyetc.net/) (Richard Yetter Chappell). The best philosophy blog out there, bar none.
-   **[Rational altruist](http://rationalaltruist.com/)** (Paul Christiano). In-depth essays about the far future. Sadly, now defunct.
-   [**Reflective disequilibrium**](http://reflectivedisequilibrium.blogspot.co.uk/) (Carl Shulman). A wide-ranging blog by possibly my favorite living thinker.
-   [Scott Young](http://www.scotthyoung.com/blog/). One of the few "productivity" gurus I respect.
-   [Shtetl-Optimized (Scott Aaronson)](https://scottaaronson.blog/).
-   [Stefan Schubert](https://stefanfschubert.com/blog). A blog by an eminently sensible man.
-   [The GiveWell Blog](http://blog.givewell.org/). Research updates by the leading metacharity.
-   [**The Polemical Medic**](http://www.thepolemicalmedic.com/) (Greg Lewis). "Renaissance man affectations from a jejune junior doctor."

_See also: [Effective altruism blogs](http://www.stafforini.com/blog/effective-altruism-blogs/)_
