_See also: [Android apps I use](http://www.stafforini.com/blog/android-apps-i-like-2/)_

These are the Chrome extensions I currently use.  My favorite ones are **boldfaced**.

-   [Application Launcher for Drive](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/application-launcher-for/lmjegmlicamnimmfhcmpkclmigmmcbeh). Open Drive files directly from your browser in compatible applications installed on your computer.
-   [Bitly](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/bitly-short-links-and-qr/iabeihobmhlgpkcgjiloemdbofjbdcic). URL shortener.
-   [BuiltWith Technology Profiler](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/builtwith-technology-prof/dapjbgnjinbpoindlpdmhochffioedbn). Find out what the website you are visiting is built with.
-   [**Bypass Paywalls**](https://github.com/iamadamdev/bypass-paywalls-chrome). See paywalled content from all the major media outlets (e.g. _Financial Times_, _The Economist_, etc.) as well as other sites.
-   [**Browserpass**](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/browserpass/naepdomgkenhinolocfifgehidddafch). Browser extension for [pass](https://www.passwordstore.org/), the standard unix password manager.
-   **[CLUT: Cycle Last Used Tab](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/clut-cycle-last-used-tabs/cobieddmkhhnbeldhncnfcgcaccmehgn)**. Toggle between your current and last used tab with a keyboard shortcut. I use this extension _hundreds_ of times per day.
-   [Contact Out](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/find-anyones-email-contac/jjdemeiffadmmjhkbbpglgnlgeafomjo). Find a person's email, as long as they have a LinkedIn account.
-   [**Dark Reader**](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/dark-reader/eimadpbcbfnmbkopoojfekhnkhdbieeh). Dark mode for every website.
-   [Don't add custom search engines](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/dont-add-custom-search-en/dnodlcololidkjgbpeoleabmkocdhacc). Prevent Chrome from auto-adding custom search engines.
-   [Get RSS Feed URL](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/get-rss-feed-url/kfghpdldaipanmkhfpdcjglncmilendn). Adds one-click subscription to Chrome's toolbar.
-   [**Ghost Text**](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ghosttext/godiecgffnchndlihlpaajjcplehddca). In combination with [Atomic Chrome](https://github.com/alpha22jp/atomic-chrome), this extension lets me enter text (e.g. writing a comment or editing a Wikipedia article) directly from Emacs.
-   [Google Docs Offline](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/google-docs-offline/ghbmnnjooekpmoecnnnilnnbdlolhkhi).
-   [Grammarly](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/grammarly-for-chrome/kbfnbcaeplbcioakkpcpgfkobkghlhen). Spell checker and more.
-   [Keyboard Shortcuts for Google Translate](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/keyboard-shortcuts-for-go/akjhnbnjanndggbcegmdggfjjclohjpo). Provides keyboard shortcuts on the Google Translate page.
-   [My IMDb](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/my-imdb/ngicopfkgbodejbbfalbmobdpjebhhmb). Highlights the movies you've already seen (voted for) when searching IMDb or displaying actor/director pages.
-   [MyEtherWallet](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/myetherwallet/nlbmnnijcnlegkjjpcfjclmcfggfefdm).
-   [Print Friendly &amp; PDF](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/print-friendly-pdf/ohlencieiipommannpdfcmfdpjjmeolj). Improves the print version of a webpage by removing ads and fixing broken layout.
-   [Return YouTube dislike](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/return-youtube-dislike/gebbhagfogifgggkldgodflihgfeippi). For inexplicable reasons, although YouTube still lets users dislike videos, it no longer displays the number of video dislikes. This extension restores the dislike count.
-   [ReviewMeta.com Review Analyzer](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/reviewmetacom-review-anal/fjifglfkcaipnmhngbigdebkoikioend?hl=en). Adjusts Amazon's ratings to exclude fake reviews.
-   **[Shut up](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/shut-up-comment-blocker/oklfoejikkmejobodofaimigojomlfim)**. "If you've ever gone to a website and read its comments section, you'll have seen that it's just a vitriolic, idiotic wasteland. Why subject yourself to that?"
-   **[Smile Always](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/smile-always/jgpmhnmjbhgkhpbgelalfpplebgfjmbf)**. Donates a fraction of the money you spend on Amazon to your chosen charity.
-   [Tag Assistant](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/tag-assistant-by-google/kejbdjndbnbjgmefkgdddjlbokphdefk). Helps troubleshoot installation of various Google tags including Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager and more.
-   [uBlacklist](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ublacklist/pncfbmialoiaghdehhbnbhkkgmjanfhe). Blocks specific sites from appearing in Google search results.
-   **[uBlock Origin](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ublock-origin/cjpalhdlnbpafiamejdnhcphjbkeiagm)**. A fast, potent, and lean ad-blocker.
-   **[Vimium](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/vimium/dbepggeogbaibhgnhhndojpepiihcmeb)**. Provides keyboard shortcuts for navigation and control in the spirit of Vim.
-   [Wayback Machine](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/wayback-machine/fpnmgdkabkmnadcjpehmlllkndpkmiak). Reduce annoying 404 pages by automatically checking for an archived copy in the Wayback Machine.
-   [**Wordtune**](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/wordtune-ai-powered-writi/nllcnknpjnininklegdoijpljgdjkijc). AI-powered sentence rewriter.
-   [Zotero Connector](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/zotero-connector/ekhagklcjbdpajgpjgmbionohlpdbjgc). Save references to Zotero from your web browser
