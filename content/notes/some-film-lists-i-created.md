Here are half a dozen or so lists of films I created on the Internet Movie Database over the past couple of years. I

- [**Borges and cinema**](https://www.imdb.com/list/ls022989414/)
  A list of films reviewed or mentioned by Jorge Luis Borges (1899-1986), as listed in Edgardo Cozarinsky's [*Borges y el cine*](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1Y0rriJ3otJRiWQ_Tt8HPtymUCc-7TJoD) (Madrid, 1981).
- [**Film soleil**](https://www.imdb.com/list/ls022775649/)
  A list of all films included in D.K. Holm's [*Film soleil*](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1MQ9gre38NYaeZ3s0lAq7jBAImNNbbTKG) (Harpenden, 2005).
- **[Borde & Chaumeton's film noir](https://www.imdb.com/list/ls063304411/) [filmography](https://www.imdb.com/list/ls063304411/)**
  A list of all films included on pp. 165-228 of the English translation of [*A Panorama of American Film Noir: 1941–1953*](https://drive.google.com/open?id=0B4kMPIEI5Mb8Q1JRQUVZa2xZeGM), by Raymond Borde and Étienne Chaumeton (San Francisco, 2002).
