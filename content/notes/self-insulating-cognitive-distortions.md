## Incompetence {#incompetence}

Dunning Kruger effect. "The less they know, the less they know it."


## Self-deception {#self-deception}

People who are deceived about themselves are usually also deceived about their self-deception.


## Attention {#attention}

"We are simply not paying enough attention to notice that we are not paying attention" (_Mindfulness in Plain English_, p. 32)


## Motivation {#motivation}

"When I was tired and had a headache recently, there was no motivational phrase or thought that was going to make me work on this book. _I didn't want to get motivated either_... There will be times in your life that you won't be motivated to get motivated." (Guise, _Tiny-habits_)


## Criticism aversion {#criticism-aversion}

Those incapable of being criticized are, as such, incapable of being criticized about it.
