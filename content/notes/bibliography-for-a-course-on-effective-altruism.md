A professor of economics is planning to give a course on effective altruism in the coming academic year, and he asked me and others for a brief list of relevant scholarly writings. Here's what I suggested [I have since revised and updated the list---PS, April 2020]:

-   Aschenbrenner, Leopold, [Existential risk and growth](https://leopoldaschenbrenner.github.io/xriskandgrowth/ExistentialRiskAndGrowth050.pdf), forthcoming.
-   Beckstead, Nick, _[On the overwhelming importance of shaping the far future](https://docs.google.com/viewer?a=v&pid=sites&srcid=ZGVmYXVsdGRvbWFpbnxuYmVja3N0ZWFkfGd4OjM4ZDVlYjY4ZTkzMzFlZjE)_, unpublished doctoral dissertation, Rutgers University, chap. 1.
-   Bostrom, Nick, [Existential risk prevention as global priority](http://www.existential-risk.org/concept.pdf), _Global policy_, vol. 4, no. 1 (February, 2013), pp. 15-31.
-   Greaves, Hilary, [Cluelessness](http://stafforini.com/docs/Greaves%20-%20Cluelessness.pdf), _Proceedings of the Aristotelian Society_, vol. 116, no. 3 (December, 2016), pp. 311-339.
-   Greaves, Hilary &amp; William MacAskill, [The case for strong longtermism](https://globalprioritiesinstitute.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/Greaves_MacAskill_strong_longtermism.pdf), forthcoming.
-   MacAskill, William, [Replaceability, career choice, and making a difference](https://www.academia.edu/1557895/Replaceability_Career_Choice_and_Making_a_Difference), _Ethical theory and moral practice_, vol. 17, no. 2 (April, 2014), pp. 269-283.
-   Matheny, Jason Gaverick, [Reducing the risk of human extinction](http://users.physics.harvard.edu/~wilson/pmpmta/Mahoney_extinction.pdf), _Risk analysis_, vol. 27, no. 5 (October, 2007), pp. 1335-1344.
-   Matheny, Jason Gaverick and Kai M. A. Chan, [Human diets and animal welfare: the illogic of the larder](http://www.jgmatheny.org/matheny%20chan%202005.pdf), _Journal of agricultural and environmental ethics_, vol. 18, no. 6 (December, 2005), pp. 579--594.
-   Ord, Toby, [The moral imperative towards cost-effectiveness in global health](http://amirrorclear.net/files/the-moral-imperative-towards-cost-effectiveness-in-global-health.pdf), Washington DC: The Centre for Global Development, 2013.
-   Singer, Peter, [Famine, affluence, and morality](http://pages.uoregon.edu/koopman/courses_readings/singer_famine_affluence_morality.pdf), _Philosophy &amp; public affairs_, vol. 1, no. 3 (Spring, 1972), pp. 229-243.
-   Singer, Peter, [All animals are equal](http://www.stafforini.com/txt/Singer%20-%20All%20animals%20are%20equal.pdf), in P. Singer (ed.) _Applied ethics_, Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1986, pp. 215-228.
-   Trammell, Phil, [Discounting for patient philanthropists](https://philiptrammell.com/static/discounting_for_patient_philanthropists.pdf), forthcoming.

_With thanks to Bastian Stern, Nick Beckstead and Lila Rieber for valuable feedback._
