One of the benefits of having a blog that is read by at least a few other people is the ability to make _public commitments_. Announcing my goals to my readers makes me more motivated to pursue them and in turn increases my chances of success. I have recently made my past donations public, and I will now announce my resolutions for 2015.

Effective altruism

-   Finish writing a book on effective altruism for a Spanish-speaking audience.
-   Register _Altruismo Eficaz_ as a legal charity in Argentina and create an associated professional-looking website.
-   Donate at least 10% of my post-tax income either to GiveWell-recommended charities, or to organizations that I judge to be even more cost-effective than the most cost-effective charity recommended by GiveWell.

Financial/entrepreneurship: make lots of money, so I can make the world a better place.

-   Sell my flat, and invest the proceeds in either an index fund or in an alternative judged to have a higher expected return on investment (or a combination thereof).

Health: stay in good physical and mental health.

-   Meditate for 20 minutes each day, for at least 310 days.
-   Exercise for 10 minutes each day, for at least 310 days.

Languages

-   Have a 5-minute conversation with a native speaker of each of the five languages I'm learning (French, Italian, Portuguese, German and Swedish) and be rated by each speaker as deserving a score of 7 or more on a 10-point scale.
-   Read in a foreign language for at least a few minutes before going to sleep, for at least 310 days.

Learning: acquire knowledge that is either instrumentally useful or intrinsically rewarding.

-   Finish all Khan Academy exercises.
-   Complete course on Statistical inference with an A- grade or higher.

Productivity: pursue my goals effectively.

-   Keep track of my dailies.
-   Keep track of my time.
-   Do at least 45 weekly reviews and 11 monthly reviews.

Social: have deep, meaningful relationships with the people I love.

-   Have sex with at least six new girls.
-   Don't watch porn.

Travel: visit exciting new places.

-   Visit at least five new cities, three new countries, and one new continent.

Pleasure:

-   Try at least three new recreational drugs.

Tango: improve my dancing and DJing.

-   DJ at a minimum of six different events (practicas, milongas, festivals or marathons).
-   Improve my dancing to a level such that I feel confortable offering my services as a paid instructor.
-   Write at least 24 posts on my tango blog.
