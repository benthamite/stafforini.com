> Went to bed reflecting on the difficulty age has in pretending. It's easier for youth &amp; middle age to cheat despair, but after 60 life's utter futility becomes cruelly obvious. The whole con is exposed &amp; you see that there is not going to be any happy ending, contentment, or fulfilment... just a waiting for death as the final, sole, and only relief.
